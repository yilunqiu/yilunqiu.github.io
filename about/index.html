About Us

Waves Waves is a fashion label which reveres British techniques, fabrics and traditions, while reinventing British style for modern purposes. We only utilise high quality pure wool tweed, twill, worsted, and organic cotton broderie anglaise fabrics, and create reversible, convertible, multipurpose clothing.

We worship nature. The tranquillity and eternity of nature has always been the thematic basis for our designs. We take a ‘100% natural and zero-waste’ sustainable approach and adopt zero-waste packaging. Clothing tags are printed on plantable seed cards, and wrapping papers are made from wildcrafted lokta tissues. The cardboard postal boxes are fully compostable. Our responsibility towards the nature is embedded in every decision we make.

We aspire to a serener, yet more adventurous way of life. Each of our collections introduces garments with timeless design and quality fabrics which will endure the passing of time. With our approach to constant development on the products, we aim to provide our customers with comfortable and durable wearing experience during their explorations on life possibilities.

About Our Partners

At the heart of Waves Waves is our commitment to supporting and sustaining traditional age-old techniques and craftsmanship. We collaborate with craftspeople and artisans across the UK to create original fabrics and delicate clothing.

Most of our finest natural fibre fabrics are produced in the UK, and some are sourced from Ireland, Italy, France and other continents. Every piece is handmade in North London by small groups of men and women who spend over 30 years of their life making couture clothing. Great dedication, attention to detail and skill goes into each garment.

Travelling around the world, we attempt to reveal individual stories on craftmanships and the pursuits of life goals from various cultures through our magazine, podcast channel and online community. We also look foward to introducing products from worldwide artistans through the From Our Partner collection.
